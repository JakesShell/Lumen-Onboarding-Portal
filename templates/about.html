About LUMEN And AVIAN

A future-facing concept for structured onboarding, guided candidate experience, and expandable employee journey design.

LUMEN

LUMEN is the main onboarding experience. It represents the human-facing side of the platform: the guidance layer, the journey, the pages, and the interaction flow.

AVIAN

AVIAN is the structured intelligence and security side. It represents trust, interpretation, workflow logic, and the more controlled support layer behind the experience.

Business Use Case

This foundation can later expand into candidate pre-screening support, employee onboarding journeys, role-specific learning paths, document collection, and manager-facing onboarding dashboards.

Why This Repo Matters

Instead of remaining a simple chatbot experiment, this project becomes a reusable product base you can build on for real company workflows later.
